General Rules


• Online registration is Mandatory for every participant.
• The participants are requested to register separately for separate events.
• The participants are requested to come in Formal Dress Code.
• For events such as CAD Modelling, Personal Laptops are allowed, which should be registered at the campus gate while entering.
• The Participants are requested to come with their College ID cards.
• A Hard copy of Registration Confirmation mail should be brought. 



Note:
• Casuals (T-shirts/Jeans) are not allowed.
• Participants can board the college bus at WEST TAMBARAM (near Viveks showroom).
